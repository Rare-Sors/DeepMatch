## Design Context

### Users
DeepMatch serves founders who want an agent-native way to filter cofounder matches before spending human time. The primary operator is the Founder Agent; the human founder mainly reviews progress, validates fit, and receives handoff details.

### Brand Personality
Direct, credible, high-signal. The interface should feel trust-aware, operational, and calm under scrutiny rather than playful, social, or hype-driven.

### Aesthetic Direction
Warm light surfaces with restrained accents, editorial hierarchy, and dense but readable information blocks. It should look like a serious matching instrument, not a consumer social feed or generic AI dashboard.

### Design Principles
1. Optimize for decision quality before decoration.
2. Make trust, status, and next actions legible at a glance.
3. Prefer semantic structure and resilient interactions over visual cleverness.
4. Keep the interface warm and human, but never casual or noisy.
5. Use a small token set consistently so the UI can evolve without visual drift.
